    Winja (VirusTotal Uploader) - Binary

    March 2, 2020
    Malware Detection, Microsoft, Uploader, VirusTotal, Winja

    Winja is a free application for Microsoft Windows that lets you find potential malware on your system. It uses the scanning engine of virtually all antivirus products in the market to give you a very precise idea of the risk posed by a particular file. Two concrete examples of using Winja: You have downloaded a file from the Internet, you want to remove as many doubts as possible.
